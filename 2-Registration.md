# Registration

Participation in the workshop **“Control Barrier Functions in Aerospace: From Foundations to Real-World Applications”** is organized through the official registration system of the **EuroGNC 2026 Conference**.

Registration for the workshop must therefore be completed via the EuroGNC conference registration platform.

**Workshop Fee:** 125 EUR

More information on conference registration and fees can be found here:  <https://eurognc.ceas.org/registration-and-fees/>

Participants interested in attending the workshop should also consult the EuroGNC overview of pre-conference workshops: <https://eurognc.ceas.org/pre-conference-workshops/>

Please note that **participants must select one pre-conference workshop**, and the number of attendees per workshop is limited.

---

## Participation & Funding Opportunities

The workshop is strongly education-oriented and particularly intended for **PhD students, advanced Master’s students, early-career researchers, and professionals working in aerospace guidance, navigation, and control (GNC)**.

To support participation, support from the **IEEE Control Systems Society (CSS)** is currently pending. If secured, workshop participants will pay a **reduced participation fee of approximately €25–€50**, which will include a **networking dinner event**. No travel or lodging support is envisioned.

Participants interested in potential funding opportunities are encouraged to **contact the EuroGNC organizing committee early**.

## Additional Information

Further details on the **technical program, invited speakers, and workshop structure** can be found throughout this website.

The workshop is organized as a **hybrid event (in-person and virtual participation)**.  
However, **in-person attendance is strongly recommended** to maximize interaction with speakers, networking opportunities, and the overall learning experience.
